UHealth Tower delivers award-winning, expert medical care close to home. Set in the heart of Miami’s health district, UHealth Tower is the flagship of UHealth – University of Miami Health System, the first and only academic-based hospital in South Florida. UHealth Tower provides research-driven, leading-edge care in multiple specialties that’s highly personalized to your needs.

24-HOUR EXPERT EMERGENCY CARE

When every minute matters, UHealth Tower is close to you and ready to offer next-level care 24/7 from board-certified emergency medicine physicians highly trained for emergency situations. We offer advanced cardiovascular and stroke care, state-of-the-art radiology and imaging for rapid diagnoses, and more.
